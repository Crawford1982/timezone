Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to TimeSync. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to collect basic, anonymous usage data (such as page views and browser types) to help us improve the tool. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) like your name or email address.

2. Cookies

We use cookies to store your consent preferences and for Google Analytics tracking. You can clear these cookies at any time through your browser settings.

3. Data Storage

TimeSync is a client-side application. Your timezone configurations, dates, and aliases are stored in the URL parameters and, if you choose to use the "Save Team" feature, in your browser's localStorage. This information is never sent to or stored on any database or server.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
