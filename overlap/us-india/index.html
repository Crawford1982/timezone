US to India time zone overlap

Best by-city meeting windows and DST-safe scheduling.

US-India is a high-intent scheduling corridor with tight overlap. New York has the most workable window, while West Coast teams need a mostly async workflow with one protected live slot.

Fast answer: For New York and India, aim for 8:00-10:30 AM New York / 5:30-8:00 PM IST depending on season.

DST impact

India does not use daylight saving time. US clock changes in March and November shift your overlap by one hour. Re-check recurring events during those weeks to avoid accidental late-evening IST meetings.
