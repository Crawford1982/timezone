New York to Lagos time zone overlap

Best US-Nigeria meeting windows with DST awareness.

This corridor is practical for remote teams if you protect the mid-window. Lagos does not use daylight saving, so offset changes only when New York switches between EST and EDT.

Fast answer: Most teams perform best at 9:30-11:00 AM New York / 3:30-5:00 PM Lagos in winter; in summer it shifts closer to 10:00 AM-12:00 PM New York / 3:00-5:00 PM Lagos.
